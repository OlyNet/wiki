
# Membership

## How can I join OlyNet e.V. as a member?

Simply send us an email to office@olynet.club and let us know your wish to join. We will
then send you back the membership application form that you have to fill out and we will
keep you updated about the further process. You will then receive onboarding from us. The
membership is of course free of charge for you

## I still have questions, how can I contact you?

Shoot us a [mail](mailto:office@olynet.club) or stop by our office during the [opening hours](/#opening-hours).
